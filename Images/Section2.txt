Where to Stay
For a city breaks, luxury getaways, weekend trips or conferences Limerick has a vast range of top quality accommodation to suit your every need.

Whether you're looking for a smart, comfortable self catering property, an elegant bed and breakfast or a top-of-the-range hotel, Limerick and its surrounding area has something for every taste and budget.

Limerick Areas
An ancient city on the banks of the mighty River Shannon, Limerick is bustling, vibrant and utterly individual. From its beautiful Georgian architecture and grand museums to its sport-obsessed locals, Limerick City will win you over with its unique charm.

Limerick for Families
There is plenty for families and children of all ages to see and do in Limerick whatever the weather or location. From tiny tots to teens, there are activities to suit everyone!
